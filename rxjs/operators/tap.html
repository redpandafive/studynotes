Tap

  • Looks at the observable values, does something with those values, and passes them along unmodifed to operators that follow it
    • Returns an observable that is identical to the source observable
  • Useful when you need to "jump out of the stream" - use cases include
    • Actions that can be considered a "side effect" for your stream
    • Logging
    • Writing to console for debugging
    • State management to set / update the store
  • Useful for logging functions or any side effects

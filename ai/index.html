Types

    • Generative artificial intelligence

ChatGPT

  • Developed by OpenAI
  • Chat Generative Pre-trained Transformer
  • A generative AI language model that interacts in a conversational way
  • Trained on a massive dataset from web scraped data, including
    • News articles
    • Scholarly articles
    • Research papers
    • Wikipedia
    • Books
    • Documentation
  • Does not perform internet searches for answers; it predicts answers based on its training data
